Calculates the service charge for a given BankAccount.

If the account was created more than 10 years ago, the service charge is the base service charge plus the management fee. Otherwise, the service charge is the base service charge.

class patterns.strategy.minimum_balance_strategy.MinimumBalanceStrategy(minimum_balance: float)¶
calculate_service_charges(account: BankAccount) → float¶

Calculates the service charges for the account.

If the account balance is above or equal to the minimum balance, the service charge is the base service charge.

Otherwise, the service charge is the base service charge multiplied by the service charge premium.

Returns:

float: The service charge amount.

class patterns.strategy.overdraft_strategy.OverdraftStrategy(overdraft_limit: float, overdraft_rate: float)¶
